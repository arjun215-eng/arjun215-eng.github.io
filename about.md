# About Canteen

Canteen is a NYC-based web3 research and technology firm.

We're a group of builders, investors, and engineers operating at the intersection of crypto, AI, and payments. We'll be launching publicly in Q1 2026.

## What We Do

We focus on three things:

- **Build products** - We ship tools and infrastructure that builders actually need
- **Invest in teams** - We back early-stage founders building the future of web3
- **Host builder events** - We bring together the best talent in the ecosystem

## Our Team

The talent at Canteen comes from leading technology organizations including:

- Coinbase
- Solana
- Protocol Labs
- Y Combinator founders
